Y2001K

This album is about the symbology and societal impact of 9/11

I made this out of hundreds of CD's from every thrift store in my city and pushing them through an old CDJ, a broken mixer and an sp303 into my 404mk2

I really love physical record digging but i'm broke as hell so CD's are the cheapest option

Plus i think its cool that this album is a reflection of people's tastes in my part of michigan throughout the years, filtered through me
